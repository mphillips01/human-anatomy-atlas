Welcome to Human Anatomy Atlas

This website will show you what you can discover with ZSpace and Visible Body's "Human Anatomy Atlas". We will break it down into three parts: usability analysis and detailed guide. The usability analysis will evaluate the usability of the hardware and software. The detailed guide will include pictures and screen shots and address everything you need to get started discovering the human body.
